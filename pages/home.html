About
SeVoAuth is a secure and privacy-preserving voiceprint authentication system with sound usability. It uses voiceprint synthesis to protect users' voiceprint privacy, and applies a hash chain to update authentication target voiceprint to successfully resist against replay attacks, spoofing attacks and adversarial attacks. We conducted a series of user tests based on a prototype system to verify the effectiveness of SeVoAuth. The test results show that SeVoAuth is accurate, efficient, secure, robust and highly usable. Compared with other existing cutting-edge works, SeVoAuth performs the best in general.

User test
